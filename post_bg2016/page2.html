BUILDING GREEN CONFERENCE 2016

Building Green 2016, an annual event held and organized by the Philippine Green Building Council, is celebrating its 10th conference on September 23, 2016 at the SMX Convention Center in Pasay City. This event will be held as part of the celebration of the World Green Building Week led by the World Green Building Council together with the 74 green building councils around the world on September 26 â€“ October 2, 2016.


This year, the World Green Building Week theme is Change Your Perspective. It aims to inform people and spread awareness about the interesting and most often untold facts about buildings, especially green buildings, that, when read or heard of, will change how people think. Making people aware of these facts will help them understand the role of green buildings in creating positive impact and huge benefits to global emissions, energy, water, health and well-being, the economy, and beyond. Building Green 2016 carries the same theme.
